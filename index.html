The best movies of this year

Day Shift

The Day is about a father named Bud Jablonski who will do anything to provide a better life for his daughter. He cleans the city's public pool, but this job is just a cover for his real job.

Prey

Prey is about a young woman named Naru who is told from 300 years ago in Comanche country and is a fierce and highly skilled warrior. Now Naru tries to protect his people from an alien predator.

Bullet Train

Express Train is about five assassins who discover that their missions have something in common and board a fast-moving bullet train.
